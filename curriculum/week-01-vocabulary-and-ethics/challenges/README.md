# Week 1 — Challenges

Challenges are slightly harder than exercises. They take 45–90 minutes each and may require you to invent your own structure (not just fill in TODOs).

## Index

1. **[Challenge 1 — Reverse complement, GC, and translate](challenge-01-reverse-complement.py)** — implement three classic sequence operations from scratch, no Biopython. (~60 min)

## How to work the challenges

- Read the prompt, then close the file and try to outline the solution in pseudocode first.
- **No Biopython.** That is the point — to write the textbook operations once, with your hands, before letting a library do them.
- Run your solution against the provided test cases at the bottom of the file.
- Compare your implementation, after you finish, to `Bio.Seq.Seq.reverse_complement()` and `Bio.Seq.Seq.translate()` from the Biopython source.
